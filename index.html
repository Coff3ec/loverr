A Place

Matthew,

People often think affection is about wanting more time.

More conversations.

More memories.

But I think it's something else.

A place that feels familiar.

A place that asks for nothing, yet somehow gives comfort anyway.

✦

you've become that place for me.

Not because you tried to.

Not because I asked you to.

But because being around you has always felt a little softer than being anywhere else.

✦

In a world that never seems to slow down,

you became one of the few things that feels easy.

Something I don't have to force.

Something I don't have to question.

Just something that quietly makes my days better.

✦

Maybe that's why I keep finding reasons to stay.

And maybe that's why, whenever I think of comfort,

I end up thinking of you.
